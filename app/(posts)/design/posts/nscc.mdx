## My Role
Director, Marketing & Design

## Overview
Founded in 2013, the National Strategy Consulting Competition and Conference is the largest undergraduate case competition at the University of British Columbia. Our organization gathers students across the world to compete and connect with each other while learning about the field of consulting. My role entailed design of externally facing visual assets and marketing materials to promote our events to students and companies alike.
